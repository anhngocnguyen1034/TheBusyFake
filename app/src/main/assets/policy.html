Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Welcome to The Busy Simulator. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The Busy Simulator is designed to work primarily offline. We collect minimal data:

  • LOCAL Call schedules and settings stored on your device
  • LOCAL Fake contact names and message templates you create
  • LOCAL App preferences (theme, language) stored via DataStore
  • LOCAL Notification history stored in local database

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data you enter remains exclusively on your device and is used solely to:

  • Schedule and display fake incoming calls
  • Send simulated fake messages
  • Maintain your app preferences and history

3. Permissions We Request

  • PERMISSION SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — to trigger fake calls at the exact time you set
  • PERMISSION USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT — to show the incoming call screen over lock screen
  • PERMISSION CAMERA — for fake video call simulation (optional)
  • PERMISSION SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW — to display call overlay on top of other apps
  • PERMISSION VIBRATE — to vibrate on incoming fake calls

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and are never used to collect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The Busy Simulator does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. Your usage data is never shared with third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room database for call history and messages
  • DataStore Preferences for settings

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for entertainment purposes only and contains no inappropriate content.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Feedback option in the app settings.
